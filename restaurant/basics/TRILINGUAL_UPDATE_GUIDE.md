## Pattern to Follow

For sections:
```latex
\trilingualsection{English Title}{中文标题}{Deutscher Titel}{}
```

For subsections:
```latex
\trilingualsubsection{English Title}{中文标题}{Deutscher Titel}{}
```

For subsubsections:
```latex
\trilingualsubsubsection{English Title}{中文标题}{Deutscher Titel}{}
```
